Ashley's Screen Time

An interview with Ashley Fletcher on her online habits.

Ashley spends a lot of time on TikTok, her most used app, to scroll endlessly when she wakes up
and to manage the account for the makeup brand she works for, Fluide. She also uses a lot of streaming
apps like Hulu and HBO Max because she loves watching America's Next Top Model while eating dinner.

“My current favorite app is Pinterest because it helps me relax when I’m feeling anxious.
I love making boards for my friends that are inspired by them, or inspiration boards for
my dream house or tattoos I want. It’s a good place to dream about things that you want
to do and get inspiration.”
—Ashley Fletcher
